PALADIN GUIDE

LAPLACE M

PALADIN

With your shield on your hand you can take a beating for your friend. Couple with a good CC and chasing skill, you excel in both PVP and PVE.

Thunder clap

Inflicts 179% attack +585 damage to the enemy in front.

passive effect: convert 10% of damage to your HP. Cooldown time 3 seconds.

Blade Storm

Deals 1080% attack +2900 damage to nearby enemies.

passive effect: reduces target attack by 30% for 3 seconds. Cooldown time 8 seconds.

Protection of light

Increase the block rate by 30% in 9 seconds and taunt nearby enemy to attack yourself, recover 15% of max HP

passive effect: increase 20% block rate. Cooldown time 15 seconds.

Consuming rage

Increases damage by 50% and reduces spell attack by 70% for 7 seconds.

passive effect: extended time for 5 seconds. Cooldown time 12 seconds.

Savage Jump

Jumping to attack the enemy in front, deals 216% attack +540 damage, 50% chance to stun enemy for 1.5 seconds

passive effect: stun probability increased by 50%. Cooldown time 6 seconds.

Holy strike

600% attack +1500 damage to the nearby enemy, stun enemy for 1.5 seconds

passive effect: increase your defense by 25% for 4 seconds. Cooldown time 9 seconds.

Pure tank build

Example of skill build focus on survivability

Signature role of paladin the tank build as the build said, use for tanking in dungeon and raid. Player need to pay attention to boss position.

Also be caution to teammate's OT, use Protection of light to pull the boss from your party member

In PVP your role will be trying to control opponent by your high survivability and control still(stun). Example combo will be raise def by Protection of light >>> Jump in with Savage Jump >>> Holy strike to stun your enemy >>> deal additional damage by Blade Storm
